Section 01

Brand Overview

Banora Chiropractic is a trusted, patient-focused chiropractic clinic in Tweed Heads South. The brand communicates clinical expertise with warmth — a place patients feel confident in, not intimidated by.

🎯

Position

The trusted local chiropractor for Tweed Heads South and surrounding suburbs. Experienced, approachable, results-oriented.

💡

Personality

Warm, knowledgeable, direct. The patient is the hero — we are the trusted guide who helps them get back to living life.

✨

Visual Feel

Clean clinical confidence. Generous white space. Navy anchors the brand. Gold is used sparingly for high-value emphasis only.

"Real people. Real movement. Real care. Not stock photos and clinical jargon — genuine expertise delivered with warmth."

Brand Ethos

Section 07

Photography & Imagery

Images communicate the human side of the clinic. Choose photos that convey trust, warmth, and real movement — not cold clinical stock imagery.

Team
Team & Practitioners Use

Real photos of Dr James Shipway and Dr Paul Cater in clinical settings. Warm, approachable. Use: about page, homepage trust section.

Real
Real Patient Interaction Use

Warm natural lighting, genuine interaction, sense of movement and care. Shows the actual clinic environment.

Hero
Hero Images Use

Full-width images with navy overlay at 60–70% opacity. White text sits on top. Ken Burns zoom effect on homepage hero.

Stock
Generic Stock Photos Avoid

Avoid stock spine/skeleton diagrams, generic "healthcare" stock imagery with obvious models, and overly staged clinical photos.

Card
Card Images Use

Rounded corners (8px / rounded-lg in Tailwind). Consistent aspect ratio within grids. Use WebP format for all web images.

Icons
Icons & Illustrations Use

Simple, line-style icons in navy or mid-blue. Blue Ghost (#EDF5FA) background on icon containers. Consistent sizing within grids.

Image Format & Optimisation Rules

Format: Always serve WebP for web. Provide PNG/JPG originals for print.

Hero images: 1920×1080px minimum, WebP, compressed under 200KB.

Card images: 600×400px, WebP, under 80KB.

Team photos: 800×800px square crop, WebP, under 120KB.

Alt text: Descriptive, keyword-aware. Include practitioner name or service name where relevant.

Loading: Hero/above-fold: loading="eager". Below fold: loading="lazy".

Aspect ratios: 16:9 for heroes, 3:2 for service cards, 1:1 for team portraits.

Section 08

Brand Voice

Every word on the site represents the clinic. Write as a knowledgeable friend who happens to be an expert — not a corporate brochure or a sales pitch.

🤝
Warm

Speak to the patient as a person, not a case. Acknowledge their pain. Show empathy first, expertise second.

🧠
Knowledgeable

Confident and informed. Explain techniques and conditions clearly without condescension or jargon overload.

✅
Direct

Don't bury the call to action. Get to the point. Short sentences. Clear benefit. One ask at a time.

🛡️
Compliant

Always hedge therapeutic claims with "may help", "can assist". Never promise outcomes. AHPRA is non-negotiable.

🌟
Humble

The patient is the hero. We are the guide who helps them get back to life. Never boastful or self-congratulatory.

📍
Local

Reference the Tweed region naturally. This is a community clinic. Patients are neighbours, not leads.

Writing Tone Examples

Write Like This

"Back pain can stop you in your tracks. Our chiropractors take the time to understand what's going on and work with you on a plan to help you get moving again."

Not Like This

"We are the premier chiropractic clinic in Tweed Heads. Our world-class treatment will cure your back pain fast. Book now!"

Write Like This

"Many patients find that regular chiropractic care helps them manage their headaches more effectively. We'll assess your individual situation at your first visit."

Not Like This

"Chiropractic treatment cures headaches. Guaranteed results or your money back."

AHPRA Compliance Rules

Always use hedging language

"may help", "can assist in managing", "many patients find relief from"

Add a disclaimer near claims

"Results vary between individuals"

Never write

"We cure...", "guaranteed results", "the best chiropractor in...", claims chiro treats non-musculoskeletal conditions

Recommend consultation

"We recommend a consultation to assess your individual needs"

Word Choices

Preferred Words

may help can assist manage support relief from experienced evidence-based individual assess gentle

Avoid These Words

cure fix guaranteed world-class the best number one treat disease eliminate proven results
