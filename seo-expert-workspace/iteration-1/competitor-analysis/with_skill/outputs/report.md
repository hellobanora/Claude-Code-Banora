# Competitor Analysis Report: Banora Chiropractic

**Date:** 19 March 2026
**Prepared for:** Banora Chiropractic, 2/44 Greenway Drive, Tweed Heads South, NSW 2486

---

## Executive Summary

Banora Chiropractic faces strong local competition from 8+ chiropractic practices within a 10km radius. The competitive landscape reveals three critical gaps where the practice is falling behind:

1. **Content and location page strategy** -- Competitors like Bone Zone Chiropractic have built dedicated location pages (e.g., `/chiropractor-banora-point/`, `/chiropractor-gold-coast/`) that capture suburb-specific searches. Banora Chiropractic has no equivalent pages and is losing visibility for searches outside "Tweed Heads South."
2. **Review volume and social proof** -- Tweed Coast Chiropractic leads with ~50 Google reviews at a 4.9 rating. Nurture Chiropractic claims 600+ reviews at 5 stars. Banora Chiropractic's review count is not prominently visible, suggesting a significant gap in social proof that directly impacts local pack rankings.
3. **Service-specific landing pages** -- Competitors like Nurture Chiropractic dominate niche searches (e.g., "pregnancy chiropractor tweed heads") with dedicated service pages. Bone Zone has individual pages for conditions like neck/back pain and sports injuries. Banora Chiropractic appears to lack this granularity.

**Top 3 Priorities:**
- Build location-specific landing pages for all service area suburbs
- Launch a review generation campaign to close the gap with competitors
- Create dedicated service/condition pages to capture long-tail keyword traffic

### 2.1 Tweed Coast Chiropractic -- HIGHEST THREAT

**Website:** tweedcoastchiropractic.com.au

**What they do well:**
- **Multi-disciplinary offering:** Chiropractic, remedial massage, acupuncture, TCM, dietetics and nutrition -- broadens their keyword footprint significantly
- **Team depth:** 4 chiropractors plus allied health staff creates scale and availability advantages
- **Blog content:** Active blog covering health topics (posture, tech neck, winter aches) driving informational keyword traffic
