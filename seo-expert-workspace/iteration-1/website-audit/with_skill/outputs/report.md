# Website SEO Audit Report: Banora Chiropractic

**Date:** 19 March 2026
**Website:** https://banorachiropractic.com.au
**Business:** Banora Chiropractic
**Address:** 2/44 Greenway Drive, Tweed Heads South, NSW 2486
**Phone:** (07) 5599 2322

---

## Executive Summary

Banora Chiropractic's website presents a clean, professional foundation but has significant SEO gaps that limit its ability to capture local search traffic. The site consists of only 5 indexed pages, has no dedicated service or location pages, and lacks critical schema markup types for healthcare businesses. A legacy listing under "Banora-Tweed Family Chiropractic Centre" on HealthEngine and Yelp creates NAP consistency confusion that could be suppressing local rankings.

**Top 3 Priorities:**
1. Fix NAP inconsistencies across directories -- the HealthEngine listing under the old "Banora-Tweed Family Chiropractic Centre" name at the same address creates confusion for Google
2. Create dedicated service pages and location pages to capture long-tail and suburb-specific search traffic
3. Implement LocalBusiness/Chiropractor schema markup to improve structured data signals

## 2. On-Page SEO Analysis

### 2.1 Title Tags

| Page | Current Title | Assessment |
|------|--------------|------------|
| Homepage | "Banora Chiropractic \| Tweed Chiropractor \| Experts in Spinal Health" | Good -- includes brand, location modifier, and tagline. Could be stronger with "Banora Point" explicitly included |
| About Us | "About Us - Banora Chiropractic" | Weak -- generic, no keywords. Should include practitioner names and credentials |
| Contact Us | "Contact Us - Banora Chiropractic" | Weak -- generic. Should include address/location keywords |
| Dr Paul Cater | "Dr. Paul Cater - Banora Chiropractic" | Acceptable but could include "Chiropractor Tweed Heads" |
| Fees | "Chiropractic Prices \| Affordable Chiropractic Fees \| Book Now" | Good keyword targeting but missing location modifier and brand name |

**Recommendation:** Rewrite title tags to follow the pattern: `[Primary Keyword] | [Location] | Banora Chiropractic`

Suggested homepage title: `Chiropractor Banora Point & Tweed Heads | Banora Chiropractic | Experts in Spinal Health`

### 2.2 Meta Description

**Current meta description:** "Over 30 years combined experience helping families. Located centrally on Greenway Drive, Tweed Heads South."

**Assessment:**
- Length: approximately 90 characters -- well under the 150-160 character ideal
- Missing key services (back pain, neck pain, sports injuries)
- Missing call to action (Book Now, Call Today)
- Missing differentiators beyond experience

**Recommended meta description:** "Expert chiropractors in Tweed Heads South with 30+ years combined experience. Treating back pain, neck pain, headaches & sports injuries. HICAPS available. Book today - (07) 5599 2322."

### 2.3 Schema Markup

**Currently present (based on skill baseline):**
- Organization
- WebPage
- BreadcrumbList
- WebSite

**Missing critical schema types:**
- **LocalBusiness or Chiropractor schema** -- This is the most important schema type for local SEO. It should include business name, address, phone, hours, geo-coordinates, payment methods, and service area
- **MedicalBusiness schema** -- Identifies the practice as a healthcare provider, triggering trust signals
- **Person schema** -- For each practitioner with credentials, qualifications
- **Service schema** -- For each chiropractic service offered
- **Review/AggregateRating schema** -- If reviews are collected on the website
- **FAQPage schema** -- For any FAQ content (which should be created)

**Impact:** Without LocalBusiness/Chiropractor schema, the website is not providing Google with structured signals about the practice's location, services, and operating hours. This directly impacts local pack eligibility and rich result appearances.

### 2.4 Content Analysis

**Homepage content assessment:**
- Covers services at a high level: back pain, neck pain, headaches, joint pain, sciatica, scoliosis, shoulder pain, sports injuries
- Mentions techniques: Gonstead, SOT, Diversified, Thompson Drop, Activator, cranial and extremity techniques

- No educational content about conditions or treatments
- No FAQ section addressing common patient questions

### 2.5 URL Structure

### Address Discrepancy

An additional concern: some directory sources reference "Banora Shopping Village, Shop 4, 3 Leisure Drive, Banora Point, NSW 2486" while the website states "2/44 Greenway Drive, Tweed Heads South, NSW 2486." If these are the same location described differently, this inconsistency must be resolved. If the practice has moved, all old listings must be updated.

### Legacy Name Issue

The HealthEngine listing under "Banora Tweed Family Chiropractic Centre" (founded 1995) at the same Greenway Drive address creates significant confusion. A separate website (banoratweedchiropractic.com) also exists for this entity. This situation means:
- Google may be conflating two separate business entities
- Citation signals are split between two business names at the same address
- Patients searching for "Banora Chiropractic" may find outdated information under the old name
- The HealthEngine listing shows no available appointments, creating a poor user experience

**Action required:** Claim and update or remove the HealthEngine listing. Clarify the relationship between Banora Chiropractic and Banora-Tweed Family Chiropractic to Google by ensuring only one active, correctly-named listing exists per platform.

## 4. Technical SEO Assessment

### 4.1 Website Platform
The site appears to be built on WordPress (based on URL structure patterns with trailing slashes and page slug conventions). This is a solid platform for SEO with strong plugin support.

### 4.2 Mobile Friendliness
Unable to run a live mobile-friendly test, but the site appears to use responsive design. **Recommendation:** Run Google's Mobile-Friendly Test at `search.google.com/test/mobile-friendly` to confirm.

### 4.3 Page Speed
Unable to run a live PageSpeed test. **Recommendation:** Test at `pagespeed.web.dev` and address any flagged issues, particularly:
- Image optimization (WebP format, lazy loading)
- Render-blocking resources
- Core Web Vitals (LCP, FID, CLS)

### 4.4 SSL/HTTPS
The website uses HTTPS -- this is correct and meets Google's security requirements.

### 4.5 Internal Linking
With only 5 pages, the internal linking structure is minimal. The navigation menu links to: Home, About Us, Dr Paul Cater, Fees, Contact Us. There are no contextual internal links within page content connecting related topics, and no service/condition pages to link to.

## 7. Summary of Key Findings

### Strengths
- Clean, professional website design
- Good homepage title tag with brand, location, and tagline
- Transparent pricing (fees page)
- HTTPS enabled
- Clean URL structure
- Multiple chiropractic techniques offered (good for content development)
- HICAPS integration for patient convenience

### Weaknesses
- Only 5 indexed pages -- far fewer than competitors
- No dedicated service or location pages
- No blog or educational content
- Missing LocalBusiness/Chiropractor schema markup
- Dr James Shipway has no profile page
- Meta description too short and lacks key services
- NAP inconsistency with legacy "Banora-Tweed Family Chiropractic Centre" listings
- Limited directory presence
- No visible review/social proof strategy
- Address discrepancy between Greenway Drive and Leisure Drive/Banora Shopping Village references

### Opportunities
- Low-competition long-tail keywords available (service + location combinations)
- Competitors like Bone Zone are ranking with location pages -- proven strategy to replicate
- FAQ content can target "People Also Ask" results
- Two experienced practitioners provide ample material for authority-building content
- Sports injury specialization (Dr Cater) is an underutilized content angle
- Saturday hours are a competitive advantage worth highlighting in content and schema
