## Important Guidelines

- Always use real data from WebSearch and WebFetch. Never fabricate competitor names, rankings, or statistics.
- Be specific — "optimize your title tag" is useless; "change your title tag from 'Home' to 'Banora Chiropractic | Chiropractor in Banora Point & Tweed Heads'" is actionable.
- Prioritize recommendations by impact. Not everything matters equally — focus on the 20% of changes that will drive 80% of results.
- Consider the Australian market context — directory sites like HealthEngine and HotDoc are important in Australia's health sector.
- Local pack ranking (Google Maps) is often more valuable than organic ranking for local service businesses. Weight GBP optimization accordingly.
- Be honest about limitations — you're analyzing public data, not accessing Google Search Console or Analytics. Note where the user should look for more data.
